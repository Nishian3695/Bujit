Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Bujit ("the app", "we", "us") is a personal budgeting tool developed by Nishian3695. This policy explains what data the app handles, where it goes, and your rights over it.

1. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All core app data (your expenses, income streams, balance, and settings) is stored exclusively on your device using Android's internal file storage. This data is never transmitted to any Bujit-controlled server and is only accessible from within the app.

2. Bank Account Data (Teller)

If you choose to link a bank account, Bujit uses Teller, a third-party financial data provider, to fetch account balances. Your banking credentials are entered directly into Teller's secure interface and are never seen or stored by Bujit. Teller access tokens are stored locally on your device in encrypted SharedPreferences. All balance fetches go through a Bujit-hosted backend that forwards the request to Teller using your token. No account data is logged or retained on that backend.

Teller's own privacy policy can be found at teller.io/legal/privacy.

3. Google Account Data (Google Tasks / Calendar Sync)

If you enable Google Calendar Sync, the app uses Google Sign-In to obtain an OAuth token and creates a task list ("Bujit") in your Google Tasks account containing your expenses and income streams. No Google account data is stored by Bujit beyond the signed-in session managed by the Google Sign-In SDK on your device. You can disconnect at any time from Settings, which removes all Bujit-created tasks and signs out.

4. Firebase Authentication

Bujit uses Firebase Anonymous Authentication solely to authenticate requests to the Bujit backend when fetching bank balances via Teller. No personal information is associated with this anonymous session. Firebase may collect diagnostic data per Google's Firebase privacy policy.

5. Data We Do Not Collect

Bujit does not collect, transmit, or sell any personally identifiable information. There are no analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or crash-reporting tools in the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Bujit is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes materially, the "Last updated" date above will be updated. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact

Questions or concerns? Open an issue at github.com/Nishian3695/Bujit/issues.

Bujit is a personal budgeting tool for informational purposes only. It does not constitute financial advice. Balance projections are estimates and should not be relied upon for financial decisions.
